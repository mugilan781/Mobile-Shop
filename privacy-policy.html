Privacy Policy

We value your trust. This policy explains how NexCell collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Last updated: July 15, 2026  ·  Effective: July 15, 2026
Section 01

Information We Collect

When you interact with NexCell — whether through our website, booking a repair service, or purchasing accessories — we may collect the following types of information:

Personal Identity

Full name, phone number, email address, and delivery address.

Device Information

Device model, IMEI (for repairs), serial number, and reported fault description.

Transaction Data

Order history, payment method type (COD / UPI), and repair service records.

Usage Data

Browser type, IP address, pages visited, and session duration on our website.

We only collect information that is strictly necessary to provide our services. We do not collect sensitive financial data such as card numbers or bank account details.

Section 02

How We Use Your Data

Your information is used solely to deliver, improve, and communicate about our services. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Process repair bookings and product orders — to confirm appointments, dispatch accessories, and update you on job status.
  • Customer support — to respond to inquiries, warranty claims, and post-repair follow-ups.
  • Service improvement — anonymised usage patterns help us identify what content and features are most useful.
  • Marketing communications — only if you have explicitly opted in to receive promotional messages (SMS, email). You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Legal compliance — to comply with applicable Indian law, tax regulations, and dispute resolution requirements.

We never sell your personal data. NexCell does not trade, rent, or sell your information to any third-party marketers or data brokers under any circumstances.

Section 03

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your browsing experience. These include:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function correctly (session management, form state, cart data).
  • Analytics Cookies: Used to understand how visitors navigate our site (e.g., page views, bounce rates). These are anonymised and do not identify you personally.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings such as language and layout preferences across sessions.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Disabling essential cookies may affect certain website features such as form submissions or booking flows.

Section 04

Data Sharing

We share your personal data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Delivery Partners: Logistics providers or courier services when dispatching an accessory order to your address.
  • Payment Processors: UPI payment gateways process transaction confirmations. NexCell does not store any payment instrument details.
  • Legal Obligations: Government authorities or law enforcement agencies when required by a valid court order or statutory mandate under Indian law.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred to the successor entity, which will be bound by this same Privacy Policy.

All service providers we engage with are contractually required to handle your data securely and only for the specific purposes for which it was shared.

Section 05

Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. NexCell implements the following protective measures:

  • All data transmissions between your browser and our servers are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols.
  • Access to customer data is restricted to authorised NexCell staff only, based on job function and need-to-know basis.
  • Physical records (repair job sheets, invoices) are stored in secured, access-controlled premises at our Anna Nagar store.
  • Internal systems undergo regular security reviews to identify and patch vulnerabilities.

Despite our best efforts, no method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure. If you suspect any unauthorised access to your data, please contact us immediately at hello@nexcell.in.

Section 06

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as is necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law:

  • Repair Service Records: Retained for 3 years to support warranty claims and dispute resolution.
  • Order and Transaction History: Retained for 5 years in compliance with Indian GST and accounting regulations.
  • Marketing Data: Retained until you opt out or withdraw consent, whichever is earlier.
  • Analytics Data: Anonymised data may be retained indefinitely for service improvement purposes.

Upon expiry of the applicable retention period, your data is securely deleted or anonymised so it can no longer be attributed to you.

Section 07

Your Rights

Under applicable Indian data protection law, you have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time without affecting prior processing.
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: Lodge a complaint with our Data Protection Officer or with the appropriate regulatory authority.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@nexcell.in with your full name and the nature of your request. We will respond within 30 days.

Section 08

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, including payment gateways, social media platforms, and partner brand pages. NexCell is not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any third-party website you visit before providing any personal information to them. Clicking on a third-party link does not mean we endorse or recommend that site.

Section 09

Children's Privacy

NexCell's website and services are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information without parental consent, please contact us immediately so we can delete it promptly.

Section 10

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or applicable law. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
  • Notify registered customers via email (if applicable).
  • Display a prominent notice on our website for 30 days following the change.

Continued use of our website or services after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
