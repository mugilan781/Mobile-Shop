Our Story

More Than a Mobile Shop

Since 2019, we've been the trusted tech companions for thousands of customers — combining genuine products with expert craftsmanship.

Our Journey Get in Touch
Founded 2019

From a Small Kiosk
to a Flagship Store

MR Mobile Hub started in 2019 as a one-person repair kiosk in Anna Nagar's tech market. Our founder had a vision: to bring honest, quality mobile services to customers who were tired of shady repair shops and overpriced accessories.

Today, we operate a premium flagship store, serve 4,900+ customers, and stock hundreds of authentic products from top global brands. Every accessory we sell, every repair we do — carries the same promise: quality you can trust.
